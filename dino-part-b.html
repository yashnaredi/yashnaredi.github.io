Part B: Digital Interaction Flow

This section focuses on building the digital flows that integrate the VA, Knowledge Base, Copilot AI, and Predictive Engagement capabilities to provide seamless customer interaction.

Key Tasks

  • Configure and test Virtual Agent (VA) flows for each line of business.
  • Integrate Knowledge Base for quick and accurate answers.
  • Use Copilot AI to assist agents and automate responses.
  • Implement Predictive Engagement to proactively interact with customers.

Considerations

  • Ensure proper routing logic based on customer input and queue availability.
  • Test fallback mechanisms if VA or Copilot cannot resolve queries.
  • Monitor analytics for engagement success rates.
Tip: Use the Genesys Flow Builder to visually design and test your flows with the Dino Park context in mind.
