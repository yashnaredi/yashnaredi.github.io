Part B: Digital Flow Design

In this section, you'll build on your foundational setup by designing customer interaction flows that integrate bots, routing, and escalation paths to create a seamless digital experience.

Flow Design Objectives

  • Implement multi-channel interaction routing for voice, chat, and digital channels
  • Configure escalation to live agents when virtual assistants cannot resolve queries
  • Incorporate data-driven decision points using customer context and history
  • Test and validate end-to-end customer journeys for quality assurance

Flow Implementation Steps

  1. Design conversational flows in Architect and link to Virtual Assistants
  2. Configure routing points for digital channels based on interaction type
  3. Define escalation triggers and assign fallback agents
  4. Set up monitoring dashboards for digital engagement metrics
  5. Conduct thorough testing with sample scenarios and iterate

Tips for Success

  • Maintain clear and simple flow diagrams for team understanding
  • Leverage Genesys Cloud's analytics to monitor flow effectiveness
  • Continuously gather feedback and optimize flow logic
  • Ensure robust error handling for unexpected user inputs

Next Steps

Once your digital flows are designed and tested, you can proceed to integrate advanced features like AI-powered insights and proactive engagement to enhance customer experience further.
